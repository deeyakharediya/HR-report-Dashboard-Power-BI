👥 HR Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze employee data, monitor workforce trends, and uncover insights related to employee attrition. The dashboard helps HR professionals make data-driven decisions by presenting key workforce metrics through dynamic and interactive visualizations.

📌 1. Project Title / Headline
👥 HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights

A comprehensive HR analytics solution developed in Power BI to visualize employee demographics, attrition patterns, salary insights, and workforce distribution for better HR decision-making.

📖 2. Short Description / Purpose

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a complete overview of an organization's workforce by analyzing employee demographics, attrition, salaries, job roles, and work experience. It enables HR teams and business leaders to monitor key HR metrics, identify workforce trends, and make informed decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

🛠️ 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Designed and developed interactive dashboards and reports.
📂 Power Query – Cleaned, transformed, and prepared raw employee data.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic calculations.
🔗 Data Modeling – Built relationships between tables for accurate reporting and filtering.
📁 Microsoft Excel – Source file containing employee data.
📄 File Formats – .pbix (development) and .png (dashboard preview).
📂 4. Data Source

Source: HR Employee Dataset (Excel)

The dataset contains employee information such as:

* Employee ID
* Age
* Gender
* Education Field
* Job Role
* Salary
* Attrition Status
* Total Working Years

The data was cleaned and transformed using Power Query before being modeled in Power BI.

✨ 5. Features / Highlights
📌 Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations. High turnover increases recruitment costs, affects productivity, and impacts overall business performance. However, identifying the factors behind employee attrition from raw HR data can be difficult and time-consuming.

This dashboard addresses that challenge by transforming employee data into meaningful visual insights that support strategic HR decisions.

❓ Key Questions
* What is the overall employee attrition rate?
* How many employees are currently working, and how many have left the organization?
* Which job roles experience the highest employee attrition?
* Which age groups are most affected by employee turnover?
* How does attrition vary across different education fields?
* Is there a difference in attrition between male and female employees?
* What is the average salary of employees?
* How are employees distributed based on their total working years?


🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
The primary goal of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor important HR KPIs in one place.
* Identify employee attrition trends across different demographics.
* Understand workforce distribution by role, gender, education, and experience.
* Support HR teams in improving employee retention.
* Enable data-driven workforce planning and business decision-making.
📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
📈 KPI Cards

Displays important workforce metrics including:

👥 Total Employees
🚪 Ex-Employees
💰 Average Salary
📉 Attrition Rate
🎓 Attrition by Education Field

A bar chart showing employee attrition across different education backgrounds, helping identify which educational fields have higher turnover.

👨‍💼 Attrition by Job Role

Visualizes employee attrition across different job roles, making it easy to identify positions with the highest turnover.

👥 Attrition by Age Group

Displays attrition across different age groups to highlight workforce segments with higher employee exits.

🚻 Attrition by Gender

Compares employee attrition between male and female employees.

💼 Total Working Years

Shows the distribution of employees based on their total years of work experience.

🎛️ Education Field Filter

Interactive slicer allowing users to filter the entire dashboard by education field for deeper analysis.

📈 Business Impact & Insights
Improve employee retention by identifying high-attrition job roles and age groups.
Support HR managers with workforce planning and hiring strategies.
Monitor important HR KPIs through an interactive dashboard.
Identify demographic trends that influence employee turnover.
Enable faster, data-driven HR decision-making using visual analytics.
