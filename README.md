# HR-report-Dashboard-Power-BI
Developed an HR Analytics Dashboard in Power BI with interactive reports, KPI cards, and visualizations to monitor employee performance, attrition trends, and workforce distribution.
Developed a dynamic HR Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to visualize workforce data and uncover insights related to employee attrition, demographics, and organizational trends. The dashboard enables HR professionals to monitor key performance indicators and identify patterns affecting employee retention through interactive and data-driven visualizations.

The dashboard includes KPI cards displaying Total Employees, Ex-Employees, Average Salary, and Attrition Rate, along with interactive filters for Education Field. It provides detailed analysis of attrition based on job role, age group, gender, education field, and total working years, helping organizations understand workforce behavior and make informed HR decisions. Data transformation was performed using Power Query, while DAX measures were used to calculate key metrics and improve reporting accuracy.

Key Features
* HR KPIs: Total Employees, Ex-Employees, Average Salary, and Attrition Rate
* Attrition analysis by Job Role, Age Group, Gender, and Education Field
* Employee distribution based on Total Working Years
* Interactive slicers and filters for customized analysis
* Clean, user-friendly dashboard with multiple charts and KPI cards
* Supports data-driven workforce planning and employee retention strategies

Tools & Technologies: Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Microsoft Excel
